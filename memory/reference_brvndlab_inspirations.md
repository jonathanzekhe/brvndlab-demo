## Scale20 (Tom Young) — benchmark principal
App custom avec: analytics (Short Form/Long Form/Business), Content Research Agent (scrape createurs YT/IG/LinkedIn + transcripts + analyse), Member Intel (score confiance, revenue, followers), Implementation Checklist avec milestones, TomAI personnalises par membre (GPT custom avec data client). Modele "digital moat" en 8 piliers: intellectual insights, IP, proof assets, time on brand, owned media, AI, community, taste.

## Jens Heitmann — approche technique

Research Radar (tracking profils concurrents dans le temps, cumulative growth views/likes/comments), Reel Radar, Gap Finder, Profile Analysis. Cote brand: Avatar Architect, Creative Identity, Bio & Profile.
